M109: Home to a Trillion Stars
This is one of my first images taken with my telescope, the image was taken with my old DSLR back in 2021, winter. I didn't have CC back then, so this image is a extreme crop. Messier 109 is a barred spiral galaxy located approximately 60 million light-years away from Earth and contains an estimated 1 trillion stars!

Exposure info: 198*110s (RGB);

Total integration time: 6.05 hrs.
